# Google Stitch Brief — NFT "Pollos" Landing Site

Copy-paste the sections below into Stitch. Hay un **prompt general** y luego **prompts por página** si quieres generarlas por separado.

---

## Prompt General (todo el sitio)

```
Design a minimalist NFT collection website with 3 pages. The collection is called "Pollos" — 50 unique hand-illustrated colorful chicken characters as NFTs on Ethereum.

DESIGN SYSTEM:
- Background: pure white #FFFFFF everywhere
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Primary text: #111111 (near-black)
- Secondary text: #6B7280 (gray)
- One accent color: vibrant warm orange or coral (to match the colorful chicken art)
- No heavy shadows, no gradients on backgrounds
- Generous whitespace between sections (lots of breathing room)
- Images are the stars — large, square, rounded corners, no decorative frames or drop shadows
- Buttons: solid dark background (#111) with white text, rounded-full, bold

NAVIGATION (all pages):
- Sticky header: brand name "POLLOS" on the left, nav links (Inicio, Colección, Crea tu Pollo) in the center, and a small "Mint" CTA button on the right
- On mobile: hamburger menu icon that opens a full-screen overlay menu
- Footer on all pages: social icons (Instagram, X/Twitter, OpenSea) centered, copyright line below

PAGE 1 — HOME (/):
Sections in this vertical order:
1. HERO: Large centered heading "POLLOS" with subtitle "50 Unique Characters / 50 Personajes Únicos". Below, one large square NFT artwork (colorful illustrated chicken character) taking up significant screen space
2. PREVIEW GALLERY: Section title "The Collection" / "La Colección". Grid of 6 square NFT artwork cards (3 columns on desktop, 2 on mobile). Each card is just the image with rounded corners, subtle hover zoom effect. Below the grid a "View Full Collection" link
3. MANIFESTO: Two-column on desktop (text left, image right), stacked on mobile. Heading "The Story" / "La Historia". Paragraph of lorem ipsum text about the art and the mission. The right side shows a collage or single artwork
4. ABOUT THE ARTIST: Centered layout. Round profile photo placeholder, artist name below, short bio paragraph in both Spanish and English
5. MINT CTA: Large centered section. Heading "Get Your Pollo". Subtext showing price "[PRICE TBD] ETH" and "50 pieces — Ethereum". Big CTA button "Mint on Manifold". Below the button, text "Launching April 16, 2026"
6. COUNTDOWN: Large countdown timer showing days, hours, minutes, seconds until April 16 2026. Minimal design — just the numbers in big bold text with labels below each
7. FAQ: Accordion-style section with 4 questions: "What is an NFT?", "How do I buy?", "How many pieces?", "What's the price?". Clean expand/collapse with a simple chevron icon
8. FOOTER: Social links + copyright

PAGE 2 — COLLECTION (/coleccion):
1. Header section with title "La Colección" / "The Collection" and subtitle "50 unique hand-illustrated characters"
2. Large grid of square NFT artwork cards — 4 columns on desktop, 3 on tablet, 2 on mobile. Each card shows: square image with rounded corners, piece name below (e.g., "Pollo #01 — El Valiente"), no borders or shadows
3. Bottom CTA: "Mint Your Favorite" button linking to Manifold

PAGE 3 — CREATE YOUR CHICKEN (/crea-tu-pollo):
1. Coming soon page — clean and simple
2. Centered layout with large heading "Crea Tu Pollo" / "Create Your Chicken"
3. Subtitle: "Coming Soon / Próximamente"
4. Short description paragraph explaining the upcoming feature (mix and match chicken traits to create your own character)
5. One preview NFT artwork image centered below
6. Email signup CTA: "Get notified" button or simple email input + submit button
7. Subtle decorative element — maybe a few small chicken illustrations scattered lightly

MOBILE: Everything must look great at 375px width. Large touch targets (48px minimum). Single-column layouts. The art should still be prominent and large.
```
